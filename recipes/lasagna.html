Lasagna

Description

This delectable dish of tomato sauce, ground beef and pasta is an italian staple. A pairing with a wine of choice is a must. The ingredients bring a hearty helping of Tuscan hospitality. This is a dish you can't live without in any gathering.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Mix italian seasoning with ground beef
  3. cook sauce until bubbling
